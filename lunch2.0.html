1. Pilau

Steps

  1. INGREDIENTS;
  2. 3 Cups rice (Preferably pishori or long-grained rice, heaped and washed and all the water drained out of the rice
  3. 2 large onions sliced into small pieces
  4. 2 medium size tomatoes grated or skinned and cut up into small pieces
  5. 2 tbsps finely grated fresh garlic
  6. 2 tbsps finely grated fresh ginger
  7. 1 tbsp tomato paste
  8. 1 & ½ tsps pilau masala spice
  9. 1 & ½ tsps salt
  10. 10 tbsps cooking oil to fry
  11. 6 cups hot water -work with the cup you used to measure the rice
  12. Optional;
  13. 1 small grated green bell pepper
  14. 3 tbsps dhania/ coriander
  15. Preparations;
  16. In a suitable clean pan you’re using to cook the pilau, add the salad oil and place it on the fire. Let the oil get hot; not smoking hot.
  17. Add the onion and fry to a golden color. Stir frequently.
  18. Add the garlic, ginger, and fresh turmeric. If you’re using turmeric powder, wait and add it with the pilau masala spice later. Keep stirring until the entire mixture turns a dark golden brown.
  19. Add the tomato at this point. Stir a little, cover, and significantly lower the heat. Let the mixture simmer for a minute or two.
  20. Stir in the tomato paste, 2 tablespoons of dhania, and the green pepper. Add the pilau masala and the salt and evenly stir the mixture. Cover again for a minute, still on low heat, to allow the ingredients to blend well together.
  21. Next, stir in the rice and continue stirring until all the ingredients are evenly blended together. Pour in the hot water and stir the mixture. Bring it to a boil. Cover the pan and significantly lower the heat. Let the rice cook on this very low heat for about 20 minutes or until most of the water in the rice is gone.
  22. Use a fork to gently turn the rice, allowing the ingredients to mix into the rice evenly, now that they tend to collect at the top.
  23. Cover again and let the rice cook for another 10 or so minutes.
  24. Gently turn your rice again with a fork then cover. Give it another 10 minutes or until the rice is ready. Garnish with the remaining dhania (coriander) and, or any other suitable garnish.
  25. Serve with a vegetable salad, kachumbari, guacamole, or with a suitable curry, sauce, or stew.

2. Rice

Preperation;

  1. Rinse the rice.
  2. Use the right ratio of water. Add 2 parts water and 1 part rice to a large pot.
  3. Bring the water to a boil. Once it's boiling, add a big pinch of salt.
  4. Maintain a simmer.
  5. Cook without peeking or stirring.
  6. Let the rice rest covered.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork.

3. Ugali

Preperations

  1. Bring water in a sufuria to the boiling point.
  2. Add maize flour in small potions as you simultaneously stir the mixture with a cooking stick.
  3. Ensure the flour is proportional to the water as putting excess flour will make your ugali hard
  4. Once your mixture attains visible smoothness of porridge, keep turning to mix it well to soften any lumps of flour that may still be present.
  5. Lower the heat to medium temperature and keep mixing until it's medium hard.
  6. Cover the sufuria for about 30sec to a minute. At this point, the meal should emit a pleasant aroma when it's properly cooked.
  7. Serve with vegetables or stew of your choice.

4. Beef Stew

Preperations

  1. Chop the ingredients. Cutting the meat and/or main vegetables to roughly the same size will help them to cook evenly.
  2. Fry your base ingredients.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients.
  4. Add your liquid of choice.
  5. Let it simmer.
  6. Season to taste.

5. Mutton Stew

Preperations

  1. INGREDIENTS;
  2. 1 Kg Mutton Stew
  3. 1 Onion Chopped
  4. 1 Garlic Clove Chrushed
  5. 1 tbsp Butter
  6. 2 Meduim Carrots Sliced
  7. 2 Medium Potatoes Cubed
  8. 1 tsp Mixed Herbs
  9. 1 tsp Vinegar
  10. 1/2 tsp Sugar
  11. 1 tbsp Beef Stock
  12. 100 ml Warm Water
  13. 1 stick Rosemary
  14. Salt & Pepper
  15. INSTRUCTIONS;
  16. In a sauce pan, over medium heat, heat the butter
  17. Add the onions, cook the onions till glazed
  18. Add the mutton and brown the meat, Season to taste
  19. Add the garlic and rosemary
  20. Brown the meat for another 5 minutes, allowing the garlic and rosemary to marinate the meat
  21. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer for 20 minutes on low heat.
